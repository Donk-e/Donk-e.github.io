Why? / How?

I found a small piece of birch wood that was in good shape while on a walk so took it home with me and thought it was the perfect size to make a spoon with, so thats what I made. I added some curves to the design to make it nicer to hold and to give it more character, it was oiled with mineral oil and wood butter which are both food safe.

Unfortunately, I sanded too much on part of the handle exposing the heartwood (the dark brown area) which I regret doing as I think it ruins the appearance. Thankfully it does no have any negative effects apart from being a lil ugly :(. It's still perfectly useable and I use it for eating ice cream. :D
